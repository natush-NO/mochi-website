About Mochi

L.Mochi is a food brand that was founded in 2024 by a single person, the founder, Ludmila. Mochi is not just a dessert; it’s a small journey into Japanese culture. Its perfect shape, delicate velvety texture, and refined taste make mochi a truly unique treat. Today, this traditional Japanese sweet is something between ice cream and a cake. The dessert’s outer shell is made from special glutinous rice flour, called mochigome. Beneath the soft, chewy shell, you'll find a creamy mousse filling with juicy fruit fillings. We use only the finest natural ingredients—professional cream cheese, fresh berries and fruits, high-quality Thai rice flour, natural Belgian chocolate, and more. Our mochi contains no gluten, artificial flavorings, or GMOs. L.Mochi: delicate, light, and fluffy, original and extraordinary. Our mochi is entirely handmade using our own exclusive recipes.
